# Provenance and reconstructions

- Recovered original scripts are placed under `scripts/` by module.
- Recovered CSV and NPZ artifacts are placed under `results/<module>/reference/`.
- Recovered PNG figures are placed under `figures/<module>/`.
- Report-like `.txt` files are placed under `docs/reports/<module>/`.
- Duplicate files with suffixes such as `(1)` or `(2)` are preserved under `archive/duplicates/`.
- Chatexport provenance is preserved under `archive/chatexports/`.
- The bundle listing from `Repo Dateien.zip` is preserved under `archive/bundle_metadata/`.
