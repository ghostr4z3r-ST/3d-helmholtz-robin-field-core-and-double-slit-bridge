# Inherited foundation

This repository builds on the earlier companion manuscript:

**Reproducible Low-Mode Ordering in a 3D Helmholtz–Robin Eigenproblem on Cube-Like and Lattice-Linked Geometries**

## What is inherited
The earlier companion manuscript is treated as establishing:
- the 3D Helmholtz–Robin field model on cube-like and lattice-linked geometries,
- the numerical reality of low-mode ordering in that model,
- the basic field/readout vocabulary,
- and the null-model discipline used to separate robust structure from easy artifacts.

## What is new here
The present repository adds:
- spectral organization under Robin variation,
- robustness of family-level organization,
- carrier / opposite / corner-edge-face coupling,
- reduction to a minimal field-core description,
- stepwise construction of measurement geometry,
- the effective detector law,
- microscopic Robin-noise support,
- and modified setup analyses used for model-based predictions.

A reader who wants to understand why low-mode ordering is taken seriously at all should start with the earlier companion manuscript and its repository. A reader who accepts that basis and wants to understand how that ordering is organized and extended into measurement geometry should start here.
