# 00_foundation_from_paper1

This module exists to make one point explicit:
**Repository 2 begins from the validated basis established in Paper 1 / Repository 1.**

It does not attempt to re-prove from scratch that robust ordering exists in the 3D Helmholtz–Robin field model. That is the role of Paper 1.

Instead, Repository 2 assumes that basis and asks the next question:
**how is that ordering organized more sharply under Robin variation, and how does that field-core organization continue into the double-slit detector build-up?**

Use this module for:
- dependency notes,
- inherited vocabulary,
- and explicit cross-references to Paper 1 / Repo 1.
